    Our Core Approach:

    The Harkness Method fosters an interactive learning environment where dialogue, rather than lectures, is at the forefront. This approach not only ensures that every voice is heard but also enhances critical thinking, collaboration, and confidence among participants, essential skills for success in the 21st century.

    • Builds confidence & communication
    • Encourages teamwork & leadership
    • Promotes critical thinking
